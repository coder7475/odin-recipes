Ramen

Description

Ramen is a classic Japanese noodle soup dish made with a flavorful broth, noodles, and a variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Miso paste
  • Proteins (e.g., pork, tofu, or chicken)
  • Toppings (e.g., scallions, soft-boiled eggs, seaweed)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Prepare the broth
  3. Cook the proteins
  4. Assemble the ramen
  5. Add toppings and serve
